# Contributing to LEMMA (LEMMA)

LEMMA is a monetary standard. Contributions are expected to be precise, technical, and respectful.

---

## How to contribute

1. Read the full specification before commenting
2. Open an issue describing:
   - The specific section
   - The invariant or rule in question
   - The concrete concern or counterexample

3. Propose changes only with clear rationale

---

## What makes a good contribution

- Identifies an ambiguity or edge case
- Proposes a clearer invariant
- Demonstrates a failure mode
- Improves determinism or safety

---

## What will be closed without discussion

- Token price discussion
- Speculation or investment framing
- Governance proposals
- Incentive or reward mechanisms
- Use-case pitches
- Marketing or ecosystem ideas

These are out of scope by design.

---

## Tone

Disagreement is expected.
Dismissiveness is not.

We are defining monetary infrastructure. Precision matters.
